What is This Website?

This is a personal Expense Management System that helps you track, organize, and analyze your spending.

The Purpose

The main goal of this website is to:

  • Track all your daily expenses
  • Organize your financial records
  • View your spending patterns with charts
  • Export your data for analysis

How It Works

1. Home Page

Add and manage your expenses. View all expenses in a table, update them, or delete them.

2. Filters Page

Filter expenses by year, month, specific date, or maximum amount.

3. Graphs Page

View pie charts by category and bar charts by month. Download your expenses as CSV or PDF.
